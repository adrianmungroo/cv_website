Publications

Leveraging Convexity on Performance Envelope to Detect and Reduce Energy Faults in HVAC Systems

Lewe, J., Aziz, I., Mungroo, A., Solano, D.
2025 ASHRAE Annual Conference
August, 2025
  • Developed system-level fault detection framework using Q-W performance curve concavity analysis to identify energy faults in district chilled water systems without extensive instrumentation
  • Established pseudo-performance boundaries from operational data using convexity points and validated with Modelica simulations of a 7-chiller campus cooling plant
  • Quantified energy savings potential across operating conditions and identified specific faults (e.g., secondary pump inefficiencies) causing deviations from optimal thermodynamic performance

Fatigue-sensitive Feature Extraction, Failure Prediction and Reliability-based Design Optimization of the Hyperloop Tube

Mungroo, A., Lewe, J.
Journal of Infrastructure Intelligence and Resilience
October, 2023
  • Developed fatigue failure framework for Hyperloop tubes integrating thermal, pod-induced, and atmospheric stresses using rainflow counting and Palmgren-Miner cumulative damage analysis
  • Identified support spacing, material ultimate tensile strength, and tube radius as critical design variables through sensitivity analysis; found underground systems 12x more resilient than above-ground
  • Performed reliability-based design optimization under demand uncertainty, determining optimal support spacing (186.6 m) and tube radius (152.2 cm) while maintaining less than 5% failure probability over five years
